Last updated: May 23, 2026

MOA Apps Developers ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Riyadh.Guide mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Riyadh.Guide is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require account registration
  • We do not collect your name, email, or phone number
  • We do not track your location in the background
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not share any data with third parties

2. Location Data

The App may request access to your device location only when you use the navigation feature (Google Maps or Waze). This location data is processed directly by Google Maps or Waze and is not collected or stored by Riyadh.Guide.

3. Third-Party Services

When you tap navigation buttons, the App opens third-party apps:

  • Google Maps — Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Waze — Subject to Waze's Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

4. Internet Permission

The App uses internet connection only to load images from Unsplash (for some location photos). No personal data is transmitted.

5. Data Storage

All app data (saved locations, preferences) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
